[Armin Ronacher](https://lucumr.pocoo.org/2026/5/26/clankers/):

> [In my last post](https://lucumr.pocoo.org/2026/5/24/pi-oss/) I used the word “clanker” as an alternative to “agent” quite consistently and probably excessively. That choice ended up attracting a lot more attention than I expected in the Hacker News comment section of that post and a number of folks had a very strong reaction: to them it sounded like a slur, in one case even something adjacent to the n-word.

> For me “clanker” is useful because it creates distance from the machine and that is a quality which is important to me. The machine is not a person, not a co-worker, not a friend, not a little spirit in the terminal. It is just a machine, a tool, and nothing more.

I wholeheartedly agree with Armin.
